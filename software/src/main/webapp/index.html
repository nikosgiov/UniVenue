About Us

UniVenue is a platform designed to simplify the process of reserving classrooms and amphitheaters at universities. Our team works closely with the University of Crete to provide a seamless experience for students and faculty. Our goal is to make the booking process quick and easy, so that educators and learners can focus on what's really important - teaching and learning.
